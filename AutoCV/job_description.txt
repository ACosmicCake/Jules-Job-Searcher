Job description
We’re seeking a highly creative AI Artist to join our client’s team—one of the fastest-growing AI Creative Production agencies in the world. This role focuses on using the latest AI photo and video tools to craft visually stunning, hyper-realistic content for premium brands in luxury, fashion, beauty, and beyond. The ideal candidate is tech-savvy, highly creative, and excited about working across a variety of industries while blending cutting-edge technology with storytelling and design. For candidates located outside of New York, a relocation fee is available.
